About Greggy

President : Greggory T. Mavicks

Born in Columbia Maryland on July 16 1979, Greggory has been a Maryland native his entire life. A Computer Science Graduate from the University of Maryland, Greggory now works as the CTO of Ubisoft, a gaming company. Greggory attends the St. Mark's Catholic Church and is a volunteer and educator at Seeds of Peace, a peacebuilding organisation. After considering the polictical state of the nation after the 2016 elections, Greggory declared his candidacy. Being the son of an immigrant himself,Greggory hopes to give wings to his ideas and solve the nations very many problems.
