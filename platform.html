The Greggy Platform

GREGGY 2020

Dear American People,

You have a choice before you, its a fundamental decision. Do you want free and debt free college education? Do you want to have a progressive economic agenda that creates job, raises wages, protects the environment and provides healthcare for all? Do you want to be a part of an open world with fair and morally sound immigration? If you answered yes, we together could be well on our way to solving the most important issues of our time which will determine the future of our homeland.

Sincerely,
Greggy

Stance on the Issue

Free and Debt Free Education
- This idea isnt a radical one. The Greggy Promise will ensure America will follow the example of Germany, Chile, Norway and Finland and offer free college education in public universities throughout the nation.
- It is my promise to you, to stop the federal government from making profit off student loans. It has been a long overdue morally wrong process that has to end.
- Americans should be allowed access to low interest rate student loans which can be cleared by work-study plans or wont need to be paid until full time employment status after graduation.
Creating Jobs and Improving Wages for Everyone
- Our country need a development and improvement strategy for our poorly maintained roads and bridges. The 'Better America Act' would double the current funding for highway and transit development and would bring in private capital to ease the strain on taxpayers money.
- The Rail network is obsolete and we have to move towards high speed rail like in countries like China and Switzerland. The 'Better America Act' will focus on converting our existing rail to energy efficent and high speed rail lines to move around people and goods more effectively.
- Our dams are deficient and the main goal of 'Better America Act' would be to imrpove our damns to better handle floods and emergency situations while providing work to many.
- With the evergrowing need to compute and access information, the boardband speeds in our country needs to be improved to cope. The 'Better America Act' will invest $5 billion to provide the neccesity of the 21st century.
Healthcare for All
- 'HealthAll' is an important Act under the Greggy Promise. With this act, Greggy hopes to abolish copay, deductibles and need to talk to healcare companies and negotiate payment of charges.
-'HealthAll' hopes to negotiate with drug companies and obtain fair proces for necessay and essential drugs.
- The Greggy promise hopes to ensure a continuous supply of medicine to even the most remotely located communities and implement a centralised cost efficent automatic prescription filling service.
Humane Immigration
-Its a Greggy Promise to allow immigrants to purchase health coverage under the 'HealthAll' act.
-Its high time we treated our immigrants with respect and dismantled deportation programs and private detention centers.
- Its the 21st century and our BORDERS DONT NEED WALLS. Legalisation can happen without the security of our walls.
- Its a Greggy Promise to ensure that humanitarian parole will work round the clock to return unjustly deported immigrants and unify broken families.
